How to Use:

  1. Register (if you don't have an account) or Login with existing credentials
  2. Copy the authentication token that appears after successful login
  3. The token is automatically stored in your browser
  4. Access the Bakong payment UIs - they will use the stored token
Note: For testing purposes, you can also manually store the token in your browser's localStorage with the key auth_token.
